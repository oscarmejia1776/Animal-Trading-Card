Cthulhu

A creature created by the famous author, H.P. Lovecraft in 1928. Cthulhu has been haunting the mind of readers ever since. Beware the call of Cthulhu, for once he is summonned there is no hope.

  • Height: 200 meters
  • Weight: 12,000 tons
  • Age: Unknown
  • Location: Beneath the Pacific Ocean in the underwater city of R'lyeh

Residing at farthest depths below the surface of the sea, Cthulhu rises to reign destruction onto mankind. An ancient celestial being, the creature embodies both unfathomable strenght as well as durability. It uses its many tentacles adorned to its face to devour unlucky souls. Its giant wings give the beast flight, goving it the ability to travel not only in water but also in air. It waits in its underwater city, waiting to be summoned again by the beasts worshippers.
